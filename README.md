# Resilify - AI-Powered Resilience Building Platform

**Resilify** is a comprehensive emotional resilience platform that combines personal tracking, AI-powered insights, and community support to help users build lasting emotional wellness.

## üöÄ Core Features

### 1. **Enhanced Pulse Check System**
- **Multi-dimensional Emotional Assessment**: Advanced emotion tracking beyond basic mood states
- **Emotion Intensity Tracking**: Measure the depth and strength of emotional states
- **Context Tagging**: Tag emotional states with situational context for better insights
- **Step-by-step Flow**: Guided emotional check-in process for thorough assessment

### 2. **AI Copilot & Real-time Stress Classification**
- **Intelligent Stress Detection**: AI-powered analysis of emotional patterns to identify stress
- **Stress Type Classification**: Categorizes stress as acute, chronic, anticipatory, or situational
- **Personalized Suggestions**: Custom recommendations based on individual stress patterns
- **Pattern Recognition**: Long-term emotional trend analysis for proactive wellness

### 3. **Contextual Nudges & Smart Notifications**
- **Timely Interventions**: Context-aware suggestions delivered at optimal moments
- **Multiple Nudge Types**: Breathing exercises, mindfulness prompts, perspective shifts, and more
- **Multi-channel Delivery**: In-app notifications, Slack integration, and calendar integration
- **User Feedback Loop**: Learn from user responses to improve suggestion relevance

### 4. **Support Circles & Community Features**
- **Create & Join Circles**: Form supportive communities around shared goals or challenges
- **Real-time Chat**: Communicate with circle members for ongoing support
- **Progress Sharing**: Share achievements and milestones with your support network
- **Group Activities**: Participate in collective resilience-building exercises
- **Privacy Controls**: Granular privacy settings for what you share with each circle

### 5. **Comprehensive Weekly Reports**
- **Emotional Trend Analysis**: Detailed breakdowns of emotional patterns over time
- **Activity Effectiveness Tracking**: Measure which activities most improve your wellbeing
- **Personalized Recommendations**: AI-generated suggestions for the coming week
- **Sharing Capabilities**: Export and share reports with trusted contacts or healthcare providers

### 6. **Privacy-First Design**
- **Granular Consent Options**: Choose exactly what data to share and with whom
- **Data Retention Controls**: Set custom data retention periods
- **Anonymization Options**: Anonymize data for research contributions while maintaining privacy
- **Export & Delete**: Full data portability and deletion capabilities

### 7. **Smart Integrations**

#### Google Calendar Integration
- **Stress Prediction**: Analyze calendar events to predict potential stress points
- **Pre/Post Meeting Support**: Automated suggestions before and after challenging meetings
- **Activity Scheduling**: Intelligently schedule wellness activities in your calendar

#### Slack Integration
- **Direct Message Delivery**: Receive private nudges and suggestions in Slack
- **Slash Commands**: Quick access to pulse checks and insights via Slack commands
- **Weekly Report Sharing**: Share wellness reports with team leads (with consent)
- **Work Hours Respect**: Nudges respect your work schedule and time zones

### 8. **Comprehensive Insights & Analytics**
- **Personalized Insights**: AI-generated observations about your emotional patterns
- **Data Visualizations**: Rich charts and graphs showing emotional trends
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor improvement over time with detailed metrics
- **Support Circle Impact**: See how community support affects your wellness journey
